Current Work

I am currently a graduate student in Dr. Catherine Haase's mammal lab at APSU. My thesis examines how ambient temperature and handling during capture influence stress hormone responses in eastern red bats. [CV; last updated 12/2025].

Previous Work

Before beginning my graduate research, I led a small mammal trapping project focused on habitat restoration in prairie and forest environments. My work emphasized species identification, field techniques, and morphometrics to understand how small mammal populations respond to restoration efforts.
